Frequently Asked Questions

What makes low-iron glass different from standard glass?

Low-iron glass contains minimal iron content, eliminating the greenish tint found in standard glass. This results in superior clarity and light transmission, making your shower enclosure virtually invisible while maximizing natural light.

How long does installation typically take?

Most installations are completed within one day. Our professional team handles everything from measurement to final installation, ensuring minimal disruption to your daily routine.

What maintenance is required for the glass?

Our glass features an easy-clean surface treatment that repels water and reduces soap scum buildup. Regular cleaning with a mild glass cleaner and soft cloth is all that's needed to maintain its pristine appearance.

What warranty do you offer?

We provide a comprehensive 10-year warranty on glass and hardware, covering manufacturing defects and installation. This includes our self-adjusting roller system and sealing components.

Can you customize the glass for unique shower layouts?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom solutions for any shower configuration. Our team will measure your space and create a perfectly fitted enclosure that maximizes functionality and aesthetics.
